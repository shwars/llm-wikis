# Labor, Roles, and Team Structure

## English Abstract

This theme covers how AI-Assisted Software Development changes who writes software, who delegates work, and how teams supervise output.

## Current Synthesis

AI-Assisted Software Development does not simply replace programmers with a single generic AI worker. Instead it changes role boundaries: more people can delegate engineering work, engineers spend more time specifying, reviewing, and designing harnesses, and teams need clearer conventions for ownership, isolation, review, and parallel execution. The near-term effect is less 'no engineers' than 'different engineering and different team topology.'
