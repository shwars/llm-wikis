# All Hands AI

## English Abstract

Open-source organization behind OpenHands and related agent-development infrastructure.

## Current Synthesis

All Hands AI is notable because it frames AI-driven development as an open platform rather than a closed product. OpenHands gives teams another route into AI-Assisted Software Development through self-hosting, SDKs, custom sandboxes, and community-driven iteration.
