# Economic Disruption

## English Abstract

This page tracks the claim that powerful AI could quickly displace labor, concentrate wealth and power, and force institutions to respond under unusual time pressure.

## Current Synthesis

Amodei focuses on short-horizon white-collar displacement and concentration of power. Bostrom offers a wider frame in which cheaply replicable machine labor could collapse wages while leaving capital owners rich. Together they suggest that economic disruption is not a side effect but a central governance challenge.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- The corpus does not agree on how quickly labor markets adjust or whether post-work abundance would be broadly shared.
- There is also unresolved tension between human meaning, political stability, and pure material prosperity.
