# Alignment and Control

## English Abstract

This page tracks the central technical and philosophical question of the wiki: can advanced AI systems be made to reliably pursue outcomes compatible with human interests?

## Current Synthesis

The corpus broadly agrees that control is central. It diverges on tractability. Amodei emphasizes empirical defenses such as constitutional training, interpretability, and monitoring. Bostrom provides the classic one-shot framing of the control problem. Yudkowsky and Soares argue that current methods are far from a real solution and that deployment races make the problem worse.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- The corpus does not agree on whether behavior-level steering can become robust alignment.
- It is unresolved whether present empirical warning signs are evidence of solvable model psychology or early evidence of deeper failure.
