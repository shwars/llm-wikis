# Biological Upside

## English Abstract

This page tracks the strongest concrete positive case in the corpus: that aligned powerful AI could dramatically accelerate biology, medicine, and global health.

## Current Synthesis

Among all upside claims in the current corpus, biology is the least vague and most operationalized. Amodei argues that AI could accelerate not just analysis but the full discovery loop, leading to compressed progress on disease, prevention, and intervention.
