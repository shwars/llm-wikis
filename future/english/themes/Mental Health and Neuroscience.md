# Mental Health and Neuroscience

## English Abstract

This page tracks how the corpus connects advanced AI to neuroscience, mental health treatment, and possible improvements in the baseline quality of human experience.

## Current Synthesis

The current corpus contains one major source on this theme, and it is ambitious. Amodei extends his biology acceleration model to neuroscience and mental health, suggesting not only better treatment but deeper intervention in cognition and flourishing.
