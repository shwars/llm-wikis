# Timelines and Takeoff

## English Abstract

This page tracks claims about when advanced AI might become transformative and how abruptly capability gains could translate into strategic or civilizational discontinuity.

## Current Synthesis

The current corpus contains strong disagreement on exact dates but broad agreement that discontinuity is plausible. Amodei explicitly entertains near-term powerful AI and rapid domain acceleration. Bostrom provides the classic takeoff and strategic-advantage frame. Yudkowsky resists exact prediction while treating the overall direction as urgent enough for emergency politics.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- The corpus does not supply one stable timeline distribution.
- It also leaves open how much of the discontinuity comes from recursive research acceleration versus deployment breadth and institutional lag.
