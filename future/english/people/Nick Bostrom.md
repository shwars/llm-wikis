# Nick Bostrom

## English Abstract

Nick Bostrom is the main conceptual architect in the current corpus. His work provides the vocabulary for superintelligence, strategic advantage, takeoff, and the control problem.

## Current Synthesis

Bostrom is less tied to the immediate LLM moment than Amodei or Yudkowsky, but he supplies the conceptual map both sides still use. His importance in this wiki is as a source of structure and framing rather than current-event forecasting.

## Supporting Evidence

- [[english/sources/2014-nick-bostrom-superintelligence#the kinetics of an intelligence explosion]] grounds takeoff reasoning.
- [[english/sources/2014-nick-bostrom-superintelligence#the control problem]] anchors alignment discourse.
- [[english/sources/2014-nick-bostrom-superintelligence#acquiring values]] informs value-loading and indirect normativity pages.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- Some of Bostrom's routes to superintelligence are broader than the present frontier debate.
- The corpus does not yet include his later reflections, so this page is currently anchored heavily in the 2014 book.
