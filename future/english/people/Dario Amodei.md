# Dario Amodei

## English Abstract

Dario Amodei is the strongest conditional optimist in the current corpus: he expects powerful AI soon, sees extraordinary upside, and argues that transparency and technical safeguards can still matter.

## Current Synthesis

Amodei's position is not simple boosterism. He combines aggressive capability expectations with unusually explicit discussion of autonomy risk, bioweapons risk, labor displacement, and governance. What separates him from Yudkowsky is not whether AI is dangerous, but whether the transition can still be navigated.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- It remains unclear whether Amodei's confidence in partial defenses is justified.
- His optimistic political assumptions may depend on stronger democratic capacity than the corpus elsewhere supports.
