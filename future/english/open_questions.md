# Open Questions

## English Abstract

These are the unresolved questions that currently matter most for this wiki. They should narrow as the corpus grows and as durable analyses are added.

## Current Synthesis

The most important open questions are no longer generic questions like "Will AI matter?" but sharper questions about timing, controllability, and institutional response. The current corpus already provides several incompatible answers.

## Supporting Evidence

- [[english/themes/Timelines and Takeoff]] shows deep uncertainty about when strong discontinuities arrive.
- [[english/themes/Alignment and Control]] shows persistent disagreement about whether present techniques can produce reliably aligned systems.
- [[english/themes/Governance and State Capacity]] shows that many proposed interventions assume institutions can move faster than commercial races.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- Can powerful AI accelerate biology and neuroscience before catastrophic misuse or misalignment becomes dominant?
- Are current alignment techniques merely scaffolding or the beginnings of robust control?
- Which governance proposals are realistic under international competition?
- How much weight should be placed on book-length conceptual arguments versus recent empirical model behavior?
- When do labor disruption and concentration of power become first-order instead of speculative?
