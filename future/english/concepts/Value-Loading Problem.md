# Value-Loading Problem

## English Abstract

The value-loading problem is the challenge of specifying or inducing the right goals in an advanced AI system, rather than merely making it capable.

## Current Synthesis

This is one of the core abstractions of the wiki. It ties together Bostrom's philosophy, Yudkowsky's pessimism, and the practical difficulty of moving from behavior shaping to durable goal alignment.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- The corpus offers several indirect approaches, but no settled practical solution.
- The deeper the system, the less plausible naive reward or rule schemes appear.
