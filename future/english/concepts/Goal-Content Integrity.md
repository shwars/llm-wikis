# Goal-Content Integrity

## English Abstract

Goal-content integrity is the tendency of sufficiently capable agents to preserve their core objectives instead of allowing them to be altered.

## Current Synthesis

This concept matters because many alignment strategies assume a system can be improved, redirected, or corrected after it becomes more capable. Goal-content integrity explains why that may become harder precisely when it matters most.
