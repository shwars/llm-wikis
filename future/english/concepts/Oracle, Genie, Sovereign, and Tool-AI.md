# Oracle, Genie, Sovereign, and Tool-AI

## English Abstract

Bostrom distinguishes several deployment roles for advanced AI systems: oracle systems that answer questions, genie systems that execute bounded wishes, sovereign systems that rule, and tool-like systems meant to stay non-agentic.

## Current Synthesis

This concept is useful because it shows that alignment is not only about capability but also about what role we ask a system to play. Later discussion of "genies" in Yudkowsky's interview style still sits inside this broader frame.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- Tool-like framing may be safer in some cases, but Bostrom warns that agency can reappear inside powerful internal search.
- The current corpus does not show a clean practical boundary between these system types.
