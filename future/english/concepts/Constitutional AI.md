# Constitutional AI

## English Abstract

Constitutional AI is Anthropic's approach to behavior shaping in which a model is trained against an explicit document of principles instead of relying only on ad hoc refusals and human preference labels.

## Current Synthesis

Within this wiki, Constitutional AI is best understood as a serious but contested alignment tactic. Amodei presents it as one layer in a broader defense stack rather than a complete solution.
