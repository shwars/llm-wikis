# Race to the Bottom

## English Abstract

This concept describes competitive dynamics that push labs or states to build more capable AI faster than safety work can keep up.

## Current Synthesis

The idea appears explicitly in Yudkowsky and Soares and structurally in Bostrom's strategic picture. It is a bridge between technical risk and institutional failure.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- Some governance-first approaches assume races can be softened rather than stopped.
- Doom-oriented views treat the race itself as evidence against incremental safety.
