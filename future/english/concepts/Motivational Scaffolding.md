# Motivational Scaffolding

## English Abstract

Motivational scaffolding is Bostrom's idea of giving an AI a temporary goal system that helps it develop to the point where a more mature and intended goal system can be installed.

## Current Synthesis

The attraction of scaffolding is pragmatic: maybe the final values are too complex to install at the beginning. The danger is that the interim system may resist replacement once it becomes powerful enough.

- [[english/sources/2014-nick-bostrom-superintelligence#acquiring values]]

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- This concept looks elegant on paper but is haunted by goal-content integrity.
- The current corpus offers no evidence that modern frontier methods have solved this handoff problem.
