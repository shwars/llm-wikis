# Responsible Scaling Policy

## English Abstract

Responsible Scaling Policy is the operational idea that frontier capabilities should trigger stronger safety thresholds, deployment restrictions, and safeguards as models become more dangerous.

## Current Synthesis

In the current corpus this is one of the clearest examples of turning abstract AI-safety concern into a lab process. It sits between pure governance and pure technical alignment work.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- The concept is only as strong as the thresholds, tests, and willingness to actually slow deployment.
- It does not answer the deeper question of whether scaling can stay governable near superintelligence.
