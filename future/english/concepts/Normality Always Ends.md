# Normality Always Ends

## English Abstract

This is the book's reminder that stable-seeming historical periods are temporary, and that civilizational discontinuity should not be dismissed just because everyday life still feels normal.

## Current Synthesis

The phrase is not just rhetorical. It supports the argument that intuition trained on continuity is a poor guide to the next transition.
