# Virtual Biologist

## English Abstract

Amodei argues that AI should not be imagined merely as a data-analysis tool in biology, but as a virtual biologist that can participate in the full experimental and discovery loop.

## Current Synthesis

This is the operational bridge between powerful AI and the biological-upside thesis. It upgrades AI from passive software into something closer to a principal investigator, lab planner, method inventor, and experiment director.

## Supporting Evidence

- [[english/sources/2024-dario-amodei-machines-of-loving-grace#biology and health]]

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- The concept assumes access to experiments, instruments, or humans who can execute the loop.
- It also assumes that research bottlenecks are insight-heavy enough for cognition to matter greatly.
