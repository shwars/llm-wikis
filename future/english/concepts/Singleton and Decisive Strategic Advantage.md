# Singleton and Decisive Strategic Advantage

## English Abstract

Bostrom's singleton idea names a world order in which one decision-making center gains enough strategic advantage to shape the future for everyone else.

## Current Synthesis

This concept is one of the key bridges between technical capability growth and geopolitical consequence. It also helps connect Bostrom's strategic thought to Amodei's concern about who controls the "country of geniuses" and to Yudkowsky's fear of one bad transition ending the game.
