# Biological Freedom

## English Abstract

Biological freedom is Amodei's term for a future in which people can exercise much more control over health, reproduction, appearance, aging, and related biological traits.

## Current Synthesis

This concept marks the point where the optimistic corpus moves beyond curing disease and into redesigning human life options. It is both a human-flourishing claim and a future source of ethical conflict around access, inequality, and norms.
