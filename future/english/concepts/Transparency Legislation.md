# Transparency Legislation

## English Abstract

Transparency legislation is Amodei's preferred early governance move: require frontier labs to disclose evaluations, safety practices, and evidence relevant to emerging risks.

## Current Synthesis

This concept is important because it reflects a governance philosophy: start with information-forcing rules that reveal whether stronger intervention is needed, rather than beginning with maximal bans.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- This approach assumes time remains to learn before catastrophe.
- Critics would say transparency is too weak if the underlying race is already near a breakpoint.
