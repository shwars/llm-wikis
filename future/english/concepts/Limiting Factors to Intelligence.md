# Limiting Factors to Intelligence

## English Abstract

This concept names the bottlenecks that survive even when intelligence becomes abundant: speed of the outside world, need for data, intrinsic complexity, human constraints, and physical law.

## Current Synthesis

Amodei's positive case is not pure accelerationism. It depends on a specific picture where intelligence rises sharply but remains partially bounded by non-intelligence factors. This is what keeps his timelines radical without becoming an instant singularity.
