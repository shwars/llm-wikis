# Moratorium and Pause

## English Abstract

This concept covers proposals to slow, pause, or shut down frontier AI development until the world has stronger technical and political control over the risks.

## Current Synthesis

Pause proposals in the current corpus are not generic caution. They arise from the stronger claim that the default trajectory to superintelligence is catastrophic and that ordinary safety iteration is not enough.

## Supporting Evidence

- [[english/sources/2025-yudkowsky-soares-if-anyone-builds-it-everyone-dies#part iii: facing the challenge]]

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- The current corpus contains strong arguments for pauses but little evidence that global enforcement is politically tractable.
- Amodei's essays implicitly compete with this concept by arguing for guarded continuation rather than shutdown.
