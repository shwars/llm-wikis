# Country of Geniuses in a Datacenter

## English Abstract

This is Amodei's core metaphor for powerful AI: millions of superhuman, fast, parallel cognitive workers that can act together like a state-scale strategic actor.

## Current Synthesis

The concept matters because it turns vague AGI talk into a concrete operational picture. Once AI is imagined as a concentrated civilization-scale capability, questions of biology, military balance, governance, labor, and autocracy all become easier to reason about.
