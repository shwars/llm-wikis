# Anthropic

## English Abstract

Anthropic is the organization most directly represented in the current corpus as a frontier lab trying to pair rapid capability development with explicit safety, interpretability, and transparency work.

## Current Synthesis

In this wiki Anthropic matters both as an institution and as an argument. Amodei's essays repeatedly use Anthropic's practices as evidence that partial defenses, transparency norms, and behavior-shaping methods are possible.

## Supporting Evidence

- [[english/sources/2025-dario-amodei-adolescence-of-technology#defenses]] discusses constitutional training, audits, and interpretability.
- [[english/sources/2025-dario-amodei-adolescence-of-technology#humanity's test]] frames frontier-lab responsibility as a civilizational issue.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- The corpus does not yet establish whether Anthropic's safety practices are robust or merely relatively better than peers.
- Much of the evidence is self-described by Amodei and therefore should be balanced with external material later.
