# Machine Intelligence Research Institute

## English Abstract

The Machine Intelligence Research Institute is the institution in the current corpus most associated with early and persistent warnings about superintelligence risk and alignment difficulty.

## Current Synthesis

MIRI's role in this wiki is historical and strategic. The organization links the early alignment-research era to the later argument that humanity may need to stop the race rather than solve alignment on the fly.
