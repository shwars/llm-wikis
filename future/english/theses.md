# Working Theses

## English Abstract

This page tracks the current high-level working model of the future-of-AI corpus. It should evolve as new sources are ingested and as contradictions become sharper.

## Current Synthesis

1. The corpus agrees that transformative AI would be historically discontinuous, even when it disagrees on exact timelines.
2. The corpus agrees that control and value alignment are central, but it disagrees radically on whether current approaches can solve them.
3. Optimistic future visions tend to depend on a hidden precondition: AI remains aligned enough to accelerate science, medicine, and governance without seizing power or enabling catastrophe.
4. Governance is treated as a bottleneck from every direction: as guardrails for optimists and as emergency braking for doomers.
5. The present wiki should foreground worldview conflict, not just catalog claims.

## Disagreements And Uncertainties

- Whether near-term frontier systems can become dangerous chiefly through misuse, autonomy, or both.
- Whether interpretability and constitutional training are genuine paths to robust alignment or merely partial controls.
- Whether a pause or shutdown regime is politically imaginable at global scale.
