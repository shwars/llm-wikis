# Timeline of Future Events - Current Corpus

## English Abstract

This table lines up the future-facing claims in the current corpus starting from 2025. It compares not just dates, but different forecasting styles: concrete windows, conditional scenarios, and refusal to give precise dates.

## Current Synthesis

Amodei supplies the clearest near-term windows. Yudkowsky and Soares are more likely to say "anytime soon is too soon" than to give a neat calendar forecast. Bostrom gives the broadest horizon: likely this century, uncertain in detail, but strategically important well before certainty arrives.

## How To Read This Table

- Amodei often gives conditional concrete windows.
- Yudkowsky and Soares give hazard framing rather than stable optimistic dates.
- Bostrom offers strategic horizon analysis more than calendar prediction.
