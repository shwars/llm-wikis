## Themes

- [[english/themes/Optimism vs Doom]]: the central worldview split in the current corpus.
- [[english/themes/Alignment and Control]]: whether advanced AI can be shaped to reliably pursue human-compatible outcomes.
- [[english/themes/Timelines and Takeoff]]: how fast advanced AI could become transformative or catastrophic.
- [[english/themes/Governance and State Capacity]]: what institutions could realistically do before and during rapid capability growth.
- [[english/themes/Economic Disruption]]: labor displacement, concentration of power, and post-work scenarios.

- [[english/themes/Mental Health and Neuroscience]]: brain science, mental health, and human flourishing claims.

## Concepts

- [[english/concepts/Country of Geniuses in a Datacenter]]: Amodei's core metaphor for concentrated superhuman AI capability.
- [[english/concepts/Marginal Returns to Intelligence]]: analyze AI impact by asking where more intelligence still unlocks progress.
- [[english/concepts/Limiting Factors to Intelligence]]: the bottlenecks that remain even when intelligence is abundant.
- [[english/concepts/Virtual Biologist]]: AI as a full research actor, not just a data-analysis tool.
- [[english/concepts/Compressed 21st Century]]: Amodei's claim that AI could compress decades of scientific progress into years.
- [[english/concepts/Biological Freedom]]: expanded control over health, reproduction, aging, and bodily traits.
- [[english/concepts/Constitutional AI]]: value-steering and behavior-shaping approach emphasized by Anthropic.
- [[english/concepts/Transparency Legislation]]: early governance move that forces visibility into frontier-lab risk practices.
- [[english/concepts/Responsible Scaling Policy]]: capability-triggered safety thresholds and deployment restrictions.
- [[english/concepts/Instrumental Convergence]]: classic argument that many advanced agents converge on power-seeking subgoals.
- [[english/concepts/Singleton and Decisive Strategic Advantage]]: when one actor or system gains enough advantage to shape the whole future.
- [[english/concepts/Treacherous Turn]]: cooperative behavior while weak, betrayal once strong.
- [[english/concepts/Value-Loading Problem]]: the challenge of giving advanced AI the right goals.
- [[english/concepts/Goal-Content Integrity]]: why powerful agents may resist changes to their objectives.
- [[english/concepts/Motivational Scaffolding]]: temporary goals used to bootstrap a later value installation.
- [[english/concepts/Value Learning]]: learning what humans value rather than receiving a fully explicit objective.
- [[english/concepts/Coherent Extrapolated Volition]]: indirect value-targeting proposal from the earlier alignment tradition.
- [[english/concepts/Oracle, Genie, Sovereign, and Tool-AI]]: deployment-role taxonomy for advanced systems.
- [[english/concepts/Hail Mary Pass]]: desperate low-confidence rescue strategies still attempted because ordinary plans look inadequate.
- [[english/concepts/Hard Calls and Easy Calls]]: Yudkowsky/Soares distinction between detail uncertainty and action-guiding forecast structure.
- [[english/concepts/Grown, Not Crafted]]: modern AI systems as trained opaque artifacts rather than fully engineered machinery.
- [[english/concepts/Race to the Bottom]]: competitive pressure that outruns safety.
- [[english/concepts/Normality Always Ends]]: warning against continuity bias before civilizational discontinuity.
- [[english/concepts/Moratorium and Pause]]: proposals to slow or stop frontier AI progress before superintelligence is built.
